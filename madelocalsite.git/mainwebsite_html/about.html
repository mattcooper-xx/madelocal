What is Made Local?

madelocal.org is a not for profit organization with one key objective. Help consumers easily find local made products.

Our goal is simple. Increase awareness of businesses and individuals that create their products locally. By doing so we provide the public with a simple way to support local business, while minimizing the carbon footprint of their purchase. You too can get involved.
Made Local. For Locals.
