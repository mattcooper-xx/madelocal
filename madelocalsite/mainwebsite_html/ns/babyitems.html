My Little Earthsling

Carrying your child in a sling creates an instant bond between the two of you. This is essential for newborns, premature and adopted babies, who need extra stimulation.

We are pleased to provide you with our handmade, adjustable ring sling that will allow everyone the chance to hold baby safely and comfortably.

Our slings make it possible for you to stay hands-free while giving your baby the attention she needs. They are easy to use and suitable for newborns right up to, and including, toddlers of three or four years (up to 35lbs).
