How you can help

In the early stages of the site we will need everyone's help to spread the word as effectively as possible. 

What we ask is that you tell as many people as you can about madelocal.org.  You can do this by joining the different initiatives we are working on.

Help us get the word out so that the site has as many products as possible! In the mean time, we will be hard at work to raise awareness ourselves. Stay tuned for a lot more ways to get involved!
